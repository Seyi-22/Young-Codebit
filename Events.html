Young Codebits Award Ceremony

Young Codebit School hosted its annual Award Ceremony, celebrating the remarkable achievements of its students in coding and technology. The event recognized outstanding projects and innovations developed by young minds, showcasing their creativity and technical skills. Proud parents, teachers, and industry professionals gathered to honor the hard work and dedication of the students, making it an unforgettable evening filled with inspiration and pride. This picture captures the essence of the night, highlighting the joy and excitement of the award recipients and the supportive community around them.

Young Codebits Women are Nation Champions!
Yound Codebits Women’s Volleyball team have achieved a major feat by winning the AoC National Championship. The team worked hard and faced several opponents, including Xaverian, Winstanley, Elliot Hudson, Notre Dame, and Hills Road colleges, to reach the finals.

Field Trip at Young Codits
The students of Young Codebit School enjoyed an enriching field trip to the Green Meadows Farm. During the visit, they had the chance to interact with a variety of farm animals, learn about sustainable farming practices, and participate in hands-on activities like planting seeds and harvesting vegetables. This immersive experience provided the students with valuable insights into agriculture and the environment, fostering a deeper appreciation for nature and sustainable living. The trip was a delightful blend of education and adventure, leaving the students inspired and with a newfound respect for the world around them.
